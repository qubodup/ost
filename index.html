About

This simple website serves as a waypost leading to freely licensed, legal textures for 3D modelling and graphical design needs.

More websites holding such art of various kinds can be found in a list on FreeGameDevWiki.

If you would like to get involved with the community or share your art, try OpenGameArt, their forum and IRC as a first stop.

Source of this website available at github.com/qubodup/ost.
